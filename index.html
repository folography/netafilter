About NetaFilter

Netafilter has been built on top of MyNeta's database which contains data of the educational qualifications, net assests owned and criminal cases filed against the Lok Sabha members in the parliament house. 16th Lok Sabhaview source

A quick guide to NetaFilter

The visualisation let's you see educational qualifications, criminal cases registared against and net assets declared by candidates at the time of filing their affidavits.

Hover over a constituency to view information about the MP of that constituency in the information box.
